About me

I love to use my Software Engineering, Data, and AI/ML skills to model business processes and help every stakeholder achieve best outcomes. I have developed a passion for extracting relevant information from structured, semi-structured (mostly HTML), and unstructured data found in Documents (PDF, DOCX, TXT format, or even scans) and Images to gain useful insights that can power decision-making, while also building the data pipeline for the ETL/ELT processes that power the Deep Learning and Machine Learning models extract the information.

I enjoy working on Natural Language Processing problems from Text classification, to Entity Recognition, Information Retrieval and Summary generation. I believe in continuous learning, be it on newer System design approaches, Parallel programming techniques or Novel ideas presented in Research Papers, and adopting them into my work.
